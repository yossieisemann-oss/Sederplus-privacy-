# Privacy Policy for Seder+

**Last Updated: November 13, 2024**

## Introduction

Seder+ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.
## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We collect the following information that you provide directly:

- **Name** - Your full name and optional Hebrew name
- **Email Address** - Used for account authentication (if you choose email login)
- **Grade and Yeshiva** - To personalize your experience and organize leaderboards
- **Learning Preferences** - Your favorite subjects, learning style, and difficulty level

### Usage Data
We automatically collect:

- **Progress Data** - XP points, level, badges earned, and streak information
- **Schedule Data** - Check-ins to daily activities (prayers, learning sessions, meals)
- **Sleep Data** - Bedtime, wake time, and sleep quality self-reports
- **Learning Data** - Daily learning goals and progress toward goals
- **Teshuva Reflections** - Personal notes and feelings you choose to record
- **Personal Goals** - Custom goals you create and their completion status

### Device Information
- **Device Type** - iPhone or iPad model
- **Operating System** - iOS version
- **App Version** - Version of Seder+ you're using
- **Unique Identifiers** - Anonymous Firebase User ID

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**
   - Track your learning progress and achievements
   - Display leaderboards comparing your progress with peers
   - Award badges and XP for completing activities
   - Send reminders for prayers and bedtime (if enabled)

2. **Personalize Your Experience**
   - Customize content based on your grade and learning preferences
   - Provide appropriate difficulty levels
   - Show relevant inspirational quotes

3. **Improve the App**
   - Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
   - Fix bugs and technical issues
   - Develop new features based on user needs

4. **Community Features**
   - Display your name and progress on leaderboards
   - Compare rankings within your shiur, grade, or yeshiva

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data is Stored
- All data is stored securely using **Google Firebase**, a trusted cloud platform
- Firebase servers are located in secure data centers with enterprise-grade security
- Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
- Data is encrypted at rest on Firebase servers

### Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures:
- Secure authentication via Firebase Authentication
- Role-based access control (you can only access your own data)
- Regular security updates and monitoring
- No storage of sensitive financial information (app is free)

### Data Retention
- Your data is retained as long as you have an active account
- If you delete your account, your personal data is deleted within 30 days
- Aggregated, anonymous statistics may be retained for analytics

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

- **Leaderboard Data** - Your name, grade/shiur, XP, and badge count are visible to other users in leaderboards
- **With Service Providers** - Firebase (Google) hosts our database and authentication

## Children's Privacy

Seder+ is designed for students in yeshivas, typically ages 13 and older. We comply with applicable privacy laws including:

- **COPPA** (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) for users under 13
- **FERPA** (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act) for educational records

If a parent or guardian believes their child under 13 has provided information without consent, please contact us immediately.

### Parental Controls
Parents and guardians have the right to:
- Review their child's information
- Request deletion of their child's account
- Refuse further collection of their child's data
